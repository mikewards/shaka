# Shaka Privacy Policy

**Effective date: June 16, 2026**

Shaka ("Shaka," "we," "us") is a recreational ocean and dive-conditions app for
spearfishing and freediving, owned and operated by Mike Ward, an individual. This
policy explains what data the app handles and why. We have written it to match what
the app actually does.

Shaka has **no user accounts**. We do not collect your name, email address, phone
number, or password.

## Summary

- No accounts, no advertising, no tracking, and we do not sell or share your
  personal information.
- We use a random, anonymous device identifier so your saved spots and your
  acceptance of these terms are tied to your install — not to your real identity.
- We do not use your phone's GPS and do not request location permission.

## Information we handle

### Identifiers — anonymous device ID
On first launch the app generates a random identifier (a UUID). It is stored in
your device's secure storage and is **not** your name, email, advertising ID, or a
hardware serial number. We send it to our server only to:
- store and retrieve the spots you save, and
- record your acceptance of these legal terms.

### Location you provide (not GPS)
Shaka does not access your device location and does not ask for location
permission. The only location data involved is information **you** enter or choose:
- a "Map Home" center point you pick, stored only on your device;
- the coordinates of spots you choose to save, which are sent to our server; and
- the coordinates of the map area you are viewing, sent to fetch ocean conditions.

### Saved spots (user content)
If you save a spot, its name and coordinates are stored on our servers and linked to
your anonymous device ID. You can view and delete your saved spots in the app.

### Diagnostics
We use Sentry to receive crash and error reports so we can fix problems. These reports
contain technical diagnostic information (such as error details and app state) and are
not used to identify you. We do not run product analytics and do not track your
behavior across apps or websites.

### Legal acceptance record
When you accept these terms on first launch, we store a record on our servers
containing your anonymous device ID, the version of the terms you accepted, the date
and time, your app version, and your platform (iOS or Android). We keep this record to
demonstrate that the terms were accepted. **We do not capture your IP address or
device user-agent for this record.**

## How your data is used
We use the above only to: provide and maintain the app's features (saved spots,
conditions, fishing intel), keep a record that you accepted these terms, and diagnose
crashes and errors. We do not use any of it for advertising or tracking.

## Service providers and third parties
- **Railway** hosts our servers and database (where saved spots and acceptance
  records are stored).
- **Sentry** processes crash and error diagnostics.
- A **land/water lookup service** receives a spot's coordinates when you save it, to
  classify the location.
- We fetch ocean and weather data from upstream sources (including NOAA, NASA,
  Copernicus, Open-Meteo, and others) and load map tiles from mapping providers.
  These requests are made using coordinates and are **not** linked to your identity,
  though, like any internet request, they include your device's network IP address.
- Our AI-generated fishing insights are produced from publicly available fishing
  reports and aggregated summaries. **No data about you is sent to the AI provider.**

We do not sell or rent your personal information, and we do not share it for
cross-context behavioral advertising.

## Data retention and deletion
- Saved spots remain until you delete them in the app.
- The legal acceptance record is retained for our record-keeping.
- Because there is no account, the simplest way to remove the data tied to your
  device is to delete your saved spots in the app and uninstall it. You may also
  contact us at the address below to request deletion of data associated with your
  device ID.

## Children
Shaka is not directed to children and is intended for adults (18+ / the age of
majority where you live). We do not knowingly collect personal information from
children under 13.

## Your California privacy rights (CCPA/CPRA)
If you are a California resident, you have rights to know what personal information we
collect, to request deletion, and not to be discriminated against for exercising these
rights. Because Shaka uses only an anonymous device identifier and has no accounts, we
identify your data by that device ID. We do not sell or share personal information as
those terms are defined under California law. To exercise your rights, contact us
using the details below. Residents of other U.S. states may have similar rights, which
we will honor where applicable.

## Security
We use industry-standard measures appropriate to the limited, non-sensitive data we
handle. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure, and we cannot
guarantee absolute security.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy. When we make material changes we will update the effective
date and, where appropriate, prompt you in the app to review the updated terms.
